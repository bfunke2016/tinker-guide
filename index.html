Our Mission
We are robotic researchers at the University of Southern California. We care about outreach and allowing younger students to share in our passion for robotics. The goal of this website is to help parents find the best platforms for their kids to learn about robots and programming.


Why Code?
There are many reasons for teaching every person how to code. Some of these include:
  • Actively building their own learning goals
  • Improving problem solving skills
  • Preparing for the future job market that will heavily demand these skills

There are also extra benefits when children learn programing through physical robotic devices
  • Physical play is essential for active learning
  • Learn about engineering design principles to build the robot.

A circular robot that you can navigate through any terrain. You can also use the Sphero in specially designed tablet and phone games
LEVEL
Beginner - Intermediate

DEVICES
